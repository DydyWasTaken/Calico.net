Why Roblox ?

Roblox looks like a kid game to many, but it's more rather than just a simple game platform, it helped me to understand alot about programming.

So i started making many things for Roblox games such as 3D models, game prototypes or exploits because it was fun and very entertaining to me.

So i focused on one game, Jailbreak which is a cop vs criminals game, Probably one of the most know game on Roblox. It looks like GTA but in a cheap version.

Despite the game being not as good as GTA, i love the style of the game and the amount of effort and work put into it (it shows that a simplistic game in some aspects can be good as a very complete game), so i really wanted to contribute to it, it could be a way to get popular too since this game has now 6B visits.

So here's on of my first submissions for the game, it's very simplistic because it needs to be in "low poly style" to match the game environement.

I posted it for the first time on twitter and then on reddit as a "submission ticket" to get it in game, since the community has to chose if it can be added or not with upvotes.

This post really started everything, even if it didn't get added, it got me alot of followers (around 60) and a lot of upvotes (around 600 upvotes), this time i knew i could do more for people.

After a year or two, i finally got my own car in the game, it became for a moment the most upvoted creation with "vehicle" flair on the r/JailbreakCreations subreddit.

For season 10 of the game it was the final grand prize to unlock, a good overall car with the design of the Bugatti La Voiture Noire, this is the creation that boosted me from 250 followers to 600 in some weeks.

This car still available in game but can't be purchased, it became a collectible/limited car.
